About Our School

With the Aim of providing excellent education to all the levels of social pyramid, the school was established in the year 2006, since then, we have been providing all the best means of education to the students.To fulfill this holy task, we have highly qualified staff, well equipped science labs, Audio-visual labs, Activity room, Music and Dance classes, vast universal library, Computer lab, and swimming pool for water activities.

Principal

Guru Kalgidhar School has become a center for excellence in the field of education within a short span of its existence. The School has attained a reputed and reliable status in the society in educational field within a short period of time and aims at the holistic development of children. The School prepares the students to meet the challenges of life and moulds them into civilized and better citizens. We help the students to identify their talents and develop it to the optimum.
